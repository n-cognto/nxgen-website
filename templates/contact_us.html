Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join NxGen?

Joining NxGen is simple! You can either fill out the membership form on our website, visit our office at the Computer Science Department, or attend one of our open events. We welcome students from all departments who have a passion for technology.

Do I need prior coding experience to join?

Not at all! NxGen welcomes members with all levels of experience, from complete beginners to advanced developers. We have structured learning paths and mentorship programs designed to help you grow at your own pace.

How often do you organize events and workshops?

We typically host weekly workshops, bi-monthly tech talks, and quarterly hackathons. Additionally, we have special events like code challenges, tech conferences, and industry visits throughout the academic year. Check our events calendar for the latest schedule.

Can I propose a project or workshop idea?

Absolutely! We encourage members to take initiative and propose project ideas, workshop topics, or any tech-related activities they're passionate about. You can submit your proposals through our website or discuss them with any of our team leaders.

Is there a membership fee to join NxGen?

We have a nominal annual membership fee of Ksh 500 that helps cover operational costs, event materials, and community resources. However, we offer fee waivers for students who demonstrate financial need, as we believe financial constraints should not be a barrier to learning.
