About Us

NxGen is a vibrant tech community at Jaramogi Oginga Odinga University of Science and Technology dedicated to fostering innovation, collaboration, and technical excellence. Our group serves as a hub for students passionate about technology, offering hands-on learning experiences, mentorship, and practical skills development in various tech domains.


Established with the vision of bridging the gap between academic learning and industry demands, NxGen provides a supportive ecosystem where members can explore, experiment, and excel in their technological pursuits.

Our Mission

To empower students with practical tech skills, foster innovation, and build a collaborative community that prepares members for successful careers in the fast-evolving technology landscape.

Our Vision

To be the premier tech community that transforms passionate students into industry-ready professionals, innovators, and tech leaders who drive positive change through technology.

What We Focus On

At NxGen, we believe in hands-on learning and practical application of knowledge. Our activities span across various domains of technology, providing members with a comprehensive learning experience.
